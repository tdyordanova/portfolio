About Me

Graphic Designer. Artist. Photographer.

My name is Tihomira Yordanova, and I am an artist, illustrator, and graphic designer. I hold both degrees in Arts and in Architecture. My interest includes creating art through traditional media such as paints, pastels, watercolors, graphic pencils, markers, as well as creating digital art by using digital programs such as Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and ipad for Procreate. Photography is a special interest of mine as I prefer to photograph landscape, abstract photography, and macro photography.
